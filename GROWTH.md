# Prism Growth Analysis

## Install Base Time Series (as of Feb 2026)

### Key Numbers
- **Peak**: ~37,000 daily active installs (Dec 19, 2021)
- **Current (2026)**: ~1,000-2,000 range — a ~95% decline from peak
- **India** dominates the user base (~58% at peak, likely still the majority)
- Other markets (US, Egypt, UK) were always small and have essentially flatlined near zero

---

## What the Data Tells Us

### 1. Classic Viral Spike + Long Decay
There was a big moment (likely a feature on a subreddit, Product Hunt, or social media) around late 2021 that drove a massive spike, followed by steady bleed-out over 4 years.

### 2. No Second Growth Engine
After the initial spike, there's been no feature or event that reversed the decline. The curve is a smooth decay with no inflection points.

### 3. India-Dependent
The user base is overwhelmingly Indian. US/UK/Egypt are negligible. This affects monetization significantly since Indian ad CPMs and subscription conversion rates are much lower than Western markets.

### 4. Retention Problem
People install, use it for a bit, then leave. There's no daily engagement hook keeping them coming back (no auto-rotation, no "Wall of the Day", no AI generation to play with).

---

## Growth Strategy Implications

### Priority 1: Retention (stop the bleed)
- **Auto wallpaper rotation** — gives users a reason to keep the app installed
- **Wall of the Day** with push notification — daily engagement hook
- **Home screen widget** — keeps the app visible without opening it
- **Fix sharing (Dynamic Links are dead)** — you literally can't go viral again if sharing is broken

### Priority 2: New Growth Driver (second spike)
- **AI wallpaper generation** — genuinely new value proposition that could drive organic growth, social sharing, and word-of-mouth
- **Setup marketplace** — unique differentiator no competitor has, shareable on social media

### Priority 3: Diversify Geography (better monetization)
- If AI generation + better English-language content discovery are added, there's a better shot at US/UK markets where monetization is 5-10x better per user (higher ad CPMs, higher subscription conversion)
- Consider localization for key growth markets

### Priority 4: Monetization Optimization
- Current India-heavy user base means low ARPU
- Credits/coins economy could improve engagement and ad revenue from existing base
- Premium features (AI generation, auto-rotation) could drive subscription conversion
- Creator payouts attract better content, which attracts more users

---

## Growth Targets for 3.0 Relaunch

- [ ] Reverse the decline curve — stabilize at current levels minimum
- [ ] Drive a "second spike" via AI generation feature launch + social/Reddit/Product Hunt campaign
- [ ] Improve Day-7 retention with daily engagement hooks
- [ ] Grow US/UK install base to >5% of total (from ~1-2% currently)
- [ ] Improve subscription conversion rate via premium AI features

---

## Bottom Line

The app isn't dying — it still has a loyal base of ~1-2K daily users. But without a new growth driver and retention hooks, the curve will keep declining. The 3.0 relaunch with AI + retention features is the play to reverse this.
